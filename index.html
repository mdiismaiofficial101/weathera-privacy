Privacy Policy for Weathera

Effective Date: June 28, 2026

Weathera ("the App") is developed by CybroTechnology. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Information We Collect

The app may collect or access the following information:

  • Approximate or precise location, only with your permission
  • Selected city and saved favorite locations
  • Weather preferences and app settings
  • Device information required for app functionality
  • Anonymous crash and diagnostic information, if enabled
  • Advertising identifiers used by Google AdMob

The app does not collect personal information such as your name, password, or contacts.

2. Location Permission

Location access is used only to detect your current location, display accurate weather forecasts, show local weather alerts, and improve weather accuracy.

Location permission can be disabled at any time from your device settings.

3. Advertising

Weathera uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect advertising ID, device information, approximate location, and ad interaction data.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. AI Features

AI-powered weather insights are generated through secure third-party AI providers. Only weather-related information is processed.

No personal conversations or sensitive personal information are stored.

5. Data Storage

Some information is stored locally on your device using:

  • Hive Database
  • SharedPreferences

This data is used to improve performance, enable offline weather, and save user preferences.

6. Third-Party Services

The app may use the following services:

  • Open-Meteo
  • WeatherAPI
  • OpenWeatherMap
  • Google AdMob
  • OpenRouter AI
  • Groq AI
  • Cloudflare

Each service has its own privacy policy.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect your information. API keys are protected through Cloudflare Workers whenever applicable.

8. Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

9. Your Rights

You may:

  • Disable location permission
  • Delete app data
  • Uninstall the application at any time

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be published on this page.
